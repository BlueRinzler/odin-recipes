PB & Yay!!

Now this is easy, like really easy. The switch is raspberry reserves and butter

Ingredients

  1. Your fav bread
  2. Peanut butter, don't be silly and get crunchy. STOP
  3. Unsalted butter, 3 tbl
  4. Raspberry preserves, 3 tbl

Directions

  1. Heat butter on medium heat till browned in saute pan, about 5 minutes
  2. Add the preserves to pan, reduce down for 3 minutes
  3. Let the preseves/butter cool to room temp
  4. Make the sandwich, if you can't make a sandwich leave
