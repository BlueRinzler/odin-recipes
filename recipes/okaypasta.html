Okay Pasta

This pasta is okay, like just okay. But a real recipe none the less..

Ingredients List:

  1. Any long noodle, 1lb
  2. Olive Oil, lots
  3. 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  4. 1 bunch of Parsley, finely chopped
  5. 1 lemon, juiced

Directions

  1. Heat a pot of water to boiling, add pasta
  2. Add garlic, lots and lots of olive oil to saute pan on low heat
  3. Once garlic is translucent, add pasta and parsley
  4. Stir for a few minutes, add pasta water if needed
  5. Add lemon to taste and eat it.
